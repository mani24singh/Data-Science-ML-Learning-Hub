# AI vs ML vs Data Science vs Deep Learning

## 1. Artificial Intelligence (AI)

**Artificial Intelligence (AI)** is the broader concept of machines performing tasks that typically require human intelligence.

### Examples:
- Chatbots (like ChatGPT)
- Self-driving cars
- Voice assistants
- Recommendation engines

AI includes rule-based systems, search algorithms, optimization, robotics, ML, and more.

👉 Think of AI as the **umbrella field**.

---

## 2. Machine Learning (ML)

**Machine Learning (ML)** is a subset of AI that enables systems to learn patterns from data instead of being explicitly programmed.

Instead of: 
If temperature > 40 → Hot

ML learns: 
Given past temperature data → Predict whether it's hot.

### Examples:
- Spam detection
- Credit scoring
- Fraud detection
- Price prediction

👉 ML = AI that learns from data.

---

## 3. Deep Learning (DL)

**Deep Learning (DL)** is a subset of ML that uses neural networks with multiple layers.

It works well with:
- Images
- Audio
- Video
- Natural Language

### Examples:
- Face recognition
- GPT models
- Image classification
- Speech recognition

👉 DL = ML using neural networks.

---

## 4. Data Science

**Data Science** is a multidisciplinary field that uses:
- Statistics
- Programming
- ML
- Data visualization
- Domain knowledge

To extract insights from data.

### Examples:
- Business dashboards
- Customer segmentation
- A/B testing
- Sales forecasting

👉 Data Science = Using data (with or without ML) to solve business problems.

---

# Relationship Summary

AI
 └── ML
      └── DL

Data Science overlaps with ML but also includes:
- Data engineering
- Statistics
- Visualization
- Business analytics

---

# Real-world Example (Netflix)

| Field | Role in Netflix |
|-------|-----------------|
| AI | Recommending movies |
| ML | Predicting what you will watch |
| DL | Learning from viewing patterns using neural networks |
| Data Science | Analyzing user behavior & improving retention |

---

AI = Entire Brain  
ML = Learning Ability
